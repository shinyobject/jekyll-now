Wine Tasting Brochures

A good friend of mine hosts private tastings. I make the brochures for the events. I'm putting these here to show that I have an above average interest in the wine industry (beer too, but we don't do regular beer tastings). I don't hold these up as great graphic design. I usually have to do them in only a couple days in order to get them to the printer. I used to print them myself, but nowadays I use Blurb. I used to do the layout in InDesign, but when the Creative Cloud subscription got too expensive for casual use, I moved to Pages on the Mac.

There's definitely flaws in the layouts, but they only needed to be "good enough". I also tried to keep the type on the larger side because most of the attendees are older and tiny print won't be readable.
